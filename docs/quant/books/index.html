    Book Recommendations

    Book recommendations

    Joachim Klement: 7 Mistakes Every Investor Makes (and How to Avoid Them): A Manifesto for Smarter Investing

    A great summary about how investors deceive themselves. Repeating a Goodreads review: An honest investment self help book that doesn’t read like a normal self help book. No secrets, magic recipes but rather logical advice backed by research and data. Highly recommended for both private and professional investors.

    Gary Antonacci: Dual Momentum Investing

    The first half is a survey of modern financial theories & practices (worth a read on its own), then a detailed description of an influential Tactical Asset Allocation strategy.
