    What’s this?

    It’s a sort of personal (and public) wiki with random thoughts with various levels of quality and elaborateness.

    It’s my alternative to a blog: I always struggled with publishing “production-ready” blog posts vs the number of drafts I have. Some, I’d love to have publish, but they were too short for a standalone post. Some, I admit I should never put a face of the internet. Nevertheless, chronological order is definitely inapproriate for most of the content of this site. A wiki is my way to break this deadlock - to end up publishing more, and worry less about it.

    It’s here for your entertainment, and for me, for reference.
